Bio

This bio was written by Nick B

Howdy, I’m Nick B.

I work as a data analyst at a public research university. I work with teams that use representative survey data to answer important questions about the public’s experiences and opinions. Prior to my current role, I graduated from a recognized Public Affairs program at a different public university. I received a Master of Public Affairs degree with focuses in policy analysis and health policy and a Bachelor of Science in Public Affairs degree with major focus in law and public policy and a minor focus in business. Despite the pretentious titles, I began my studies at the local community college and I just didn’t stop till I was buried in student loan debt.

If I were to go back for more debt, my thesis would be the following:

A people who are not systemically and increasingly deprived of fundamental rights would not tolerate the systemic ignorance we are seeing in our public officials today

I’ve done the door-to-door work, I’ve seen the public opinion polling, and I’ve studied these issues. There are real answers that we all agree on that our elected representatives ignore at the behest of their owners. They only get away with it by depriving the people they represent of the electoral power, financial security, and civic education needed to fight back.

This project is my attempt to fight back. hope you stick around. Give me a chance to prove my thesis. Nothing to lose. Everything to gain.
