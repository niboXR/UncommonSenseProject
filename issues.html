Goal 1: Provide accurate common sense breakdowns of the issues

Defining accuracy

Objective accuracy has been obscured by the powers that be to protect themselves from an informed populace. News media is unaccountable and the internet is infinite. New technologies such as AI are poised to profoundly exacerbate a collective disillusionment. Despite this bombardment, truth is now more obtainable than ever. The internet, for all of its harm, gives us the ability to track information to original sources and determine their validity objectively.

I will frequently cite legislation and legal cases as background fact and research from governmental and research bodies as supporting evidence. For references to broad topics or specific events, I will likely make full use of Wikipedia because it is an outstanding resource maintained by a global community. As with all of the other sources, treat the information on Wikipedia with contextual understanding. False information can be written anywhere. It is our jobs to assess the likelihood of truth and appropriately apply it to our broad understanding, with no one source serving as gospel.
