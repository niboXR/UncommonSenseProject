Goal 3: Propose new solutions when necessary

The political establishment has routinely failed to implement well-established solutions to the issues we face. It is likely that unconventional thinking will be needed to fundamentally address the needs of people.

Examples of new solutions include establishing a universal basic income and redesigning the medical patent system to incentivize people over profit.
