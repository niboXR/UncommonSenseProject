Welcome to the Uncommon Sense Project

A U.S-based rights project

This project is dedicated to pointing out the nonsense from our elected leaders and discussing or discovering sensible solutions to issues of the people.

The goal is to provide accurate common sense breakdowns of the issues, document known solutions, and research untested solutions.

The philosophy is to evaluate issues and solutions in terms of how they impact every person’s ability to pursue life, liberty, and happiness. By establishing this baseline, we can avoid the fallacies of classical thinking.
