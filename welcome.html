Welcome to the Uncommon Sense Project

A U.S-based rights project

This project is dedicated to pointing out the nonsense from our elected leaders and discussing the sensible solutions to issues of the people.

The goal is to provide accurate common sense breakdowns of the issues, document known solutions, and propose new solutions when necessary.

Our philosophy is to evaluate issues and solutions in terms of how they impact every person’s ability to pursue life, liberty, and happiness.
